# Getting Started with Ally

Get up and running with Ally in minutes.

## Installation

### macOS

1. Download Ally from the Mac App Store
2. Launch Ally from your Applications folder
3. Grant permissions (microphone, screen recording)

### iOS

1. Download Ally from the iOS App Store
2. Launch Ally
3. Grant microphone permissions

## Initial Setup

### First Launch

When you first open Ally:

1. **Choose your storage location** - Where to save your knowledge vault
2. **Configure AI models** - Download transcription models
3. **Set up keyboard shortcuts** - Customize quick capture shortcuts

### Permissions

Ally requires certain permissions to function:

- **Microphone** - For voice recording and transcription
- **Screen Recording** (macOS) - For screen capture features
- **Notifications** - For recording status and reminders
