# Welcome to Ally

Ally is a privacy-first knowledge management app that captures your thoughts, transcribes your voice, and builds a personal knowledge graph—all powered by local AI.

## Quick Start

- [Install Ally](/docs/getting-started) - Download and set up on macOS and iOS
- [Your First Recording](/docs/getting-started/first-recording) - Start capturing knowledge
- [Explore Features](/docs/features) - Discover what Ally can do

## What makes Ally different?

**Privacy-first:** All AI processing happens locally on your device. Your data never leaves your Mac or iPhone.

**Multi-modal capture:** Voice, text, screen, and files—capture knowledge however it comes to you.

**Intelligent organization:** AI automatically extracts insights, builds connections, and helps you find what you need.
