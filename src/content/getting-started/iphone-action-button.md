Ally is focused on frictionless "Quick Capture." The idea is that the easier it is for you to add things to Ally throughout your day, the more likely you'll be to do it so it will be a productivity tool you actually stick with. 

Hands down, the fastest easiest way to add information to Ally is with the iOS Action button. With it, you can instantly start a voice note or meeting recording with just a button press, even if your phone is locked. 

## How to configure the iPhone Action button to start an Ally recording:

1. On your iPhone, go to `Settings.'

2. Scroll down to find Action Button in the list and tap it to open its options.

3. Swipe right to left to scroll through the options until you reach Shortcut.

4. Press the selector and search the available shortcuts for `Ally.` You'll see the option to `Start recording.` Press it to select and exit Settings. 

## How to use the iPhone Action button to start an Ally recording:

1. Press and hold the Action button on your phone to start a recording. 

2. Recordings will automatically appear in your Inbox when transcription completes (usually near instantly).


That's it! You've just eliminated all friction involved with getting things out of your head and into Ally. Now you can quickly add tasks and reminders, think outloud to brain dump before drafting a document, and so much more.
